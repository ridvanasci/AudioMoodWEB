Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

ReedOne ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use AudioMood.

1. Information We Collect

AudioMood collects the following types of information:

  • Voice Recordings: Audio recorded through the microphone is transcribed locally and stored on your device.
  • Journal Entries: Text transcripts, AI-generated summaries, mood data, and tags are stored locally on your device.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics data collected via Firebase Analytics to improve the app experience.

2. Microphone Permission

AudioMood requires access to your device's microphone solely for the purpose of recording voice journal entries. We do not record audio in the background or without your explicit action.

3. AI Processing

Your journal transcripts are sent to third-party AI services (Groq API) for analysis including mood detection, summarization, and tag generation. This data is processed according to Groq's privacy policy and is not stored by us on external servers.

4. Data Storage

All journal entries, recordings, and personal data are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. We do not upload your personal journal content to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Remote configuration and anonymous analytics
  • Groq API: AI text analysis and mood detection
  • Google AdMob: Displaying advertisements

6. Advertising

AudioMood uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

AudioMood is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted local storage and secure PIN protection to safeguard your data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
